About us!

Arribah! Salon first established in February 08, 2014 and had it's first grand opening based at Sta. Rosa Laguna and has been serving in the Salon and beauty industry for 10 years ongoing. We provide affordable yet beautiful results in Hair and Nail services for both Men and Women.

Our salon offers the perfect blend of relaxation and elegance, making every visit a special treat.

Services

Check our Services

Rebond

Get sleek, straight, and beautifully manageable hair with our professional rebonding service. Using high-quality products, we ensure your hair remains healthy, shiny, and frizz-free.

Hand Spa & Foot Spa (Free Manicure & Pedicure)

Indulge in a soothing hand and foot spa experience that restores moisture, softness, and relaxation. Enjoy the bonus of a complimentary manicure and pedicure to leave your hands and feet perfectly polished.

Hand Paraffin & Foot Paraffin (Free Manicure & Pedicure)

Pamper your hands and feet with our paraffin treatments, which deeply moisturize and revitalize your skin. This service includes a complimentary manicure and pedicure for the ultimate care and refinement.

Gel Manicure & Gel Pedicure (Free Foot Spa)

Enjoy long-lasting, flawless nails with our gel manicure and pedicure services. As an added treat, we include a free foot spa to complete your pampering session.

Color & Brazilian

Transform your look with our expert hair coloring and Brazilian treatment services. From vibrant hues to natural shades, we customize every session to match your style while ensuring smooth, frizz-free hair.

Distributor of Professional Beauty Products

Arribah Salon is also your trusted distributor of high-quality beauty products. From professional hair care to nail essentials, we offer a curated selection of premium items used and loved by beauty professionals. Stock up on your favorite products during your visit or contact us to explore our full range.
